About Me

Hi I'm Alix, my background is actually in horticulture, I spent the last 10 years managing Sloat Garden Center. Plants have been a hobby of mine for a long time but I needed a new challenge and I would like to turn plants back into a hobby versus my line of work. I figured if I could learn the latin names for most species of plants in California, I could learn how to code. This class is definitely a challenge for me, I'm going to be honest and say I'm struggling through this first project. I've never even looked at code before the pre-work but I know with time and repetition I will get this. My first vegetable garden was the same way, I didn't give up and now I'm a great gardener, I hope to apply that mentality to this endeavor.
